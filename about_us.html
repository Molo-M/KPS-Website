Meet the Team at KPS:

Welcome to the Kabuga Presbyterian School family, where our team of educators and staff are devoted to nurturing a love for learning and growth in every child. With a focus on academic excellence and moral values, our dedicated team is here to guide and support our students throughout their educational journey.

Director

Our Director is the visionary leader of Presbyterian Primary School. Responsible for overseeing the overall academic and administrative functioning, the Principal provides guidance and direction to ensure a conducive learning environment. They work closely with faculty, staff, students, and parents to uphold the school's values and educational standards.

Deputy Director

The Vice Director supports the Principal in overseeing school operations and decision-making, acting as the second-in-command. They collaborate closely with staff, parents, and students to maintain high standards and ensure smooth functioning in the Principal's absence.

Accountant

The Accountant manages the school's finances, handling transactions, financial statements, and reports. They ensure financial compliance and work with the administrative team to maintain the school's fiscal health.

Head of Worship

As the Head of Worship/Religion, this staff member fosters spiritual growth and religious understanding among students. They oversee religious activities, services, and education, ensuring students connect with the school's religious ethos.

Librarian

The Librarian is dedicated to promoting a culture of reading and research within the school. They manage the school library, curate a diverse collection of books and resources, and provide guidance to students in developing their reading habits and research skills.
