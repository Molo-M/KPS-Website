Planting Hope, Growing Change: Our Tree-Nurturing Tradition

At our school, we emphasize responsibility and environmental awareness. As part of this, every student participates in our Tree Planting Initiative, planting and nurturing a tree throughout their academic journey. This hands-on experience instills the importance of caring for the environment and the role trees play in combating climate change

The Tree Planting Initiative serves both the environment and education. Students learn about carbon sequestration, the impact of their actions on the ecosystem, and cultivate a lifelong love for nature. It empowers them to actively address climate change, laying the foundation for a brighter and more sustainable future.
